Method selection and planning

Click here to proceed to Plan1.pdf

Overall, the plan for the project consists of a desgin phase and implementation phase, seperated by a short break. Using Agile methodology, SCRUM to achieve weekly sprints followed by weekly meetings and gantt chart updates. Our team structure consisted of nominal leader who organises the weekly meetings and sorts out the agenda during those meetings. We followed the risks described in the next session to ensure that allocations pose the least number of risks possible. Our systematic plan outlines dates when activities are to start and their completion dates. See more details in the document.

Risk assessment and mitigation

Click here to proceed to Risk1.pdf

One of the largest components of project management is risk management. Hence, we have adopted a risk management strategy which decomposes the process into four main stages:
Risk Identification: To identify the risks and categorising them by type.
Risk Analysis: Weighing up and prioritising the likelihood and consequences of the risks
Risk Planning: Taking up plans to reduce the likelihood and/or severity of those risks to minimise the impact on the project
Risk Monitoring: Regularly assessing the risk and reviewing plans as the knowledge and context of the risk becomes more clear.
We believe this will be a good strategy for our team to use as it decomposes the risk management process into 4 clear steps that can be followed

Gantt Chart

Here you can see the progression of the project week by week.

^ Term 1 Week 8: 10-11-2022
After the assessent was realeased and we had established a communition method, team identity and roles. Starting to use plantUML to make Gantt charts for the following weeks.

^ Term 1 Week 9: 17-11-2022
The followin week, we began work on eliciting requirements. This included preparing a list of questions and studying the product brief. On the side, we researched game engines and website templaces (as this would need to be updated weekly)

^ Term 1 Week 10: 24-11-2022
After completing the interview, we used a recording and notes to deciifer and format user and system reqirements. We began formatting the User Requiurements, Functional Requirements and Non-functional requirements using a consistent naming scheme, in order to reference them in future documentation. This was split among the whole group in order for everyone to familiarise themselves with the expectations. With the intention to start Risk and Planning in the following week.

^ Winter Break Week 1: 01-12-2022
As intended Risk Management began on this first week due to be completed the following week. However, due to staff unavailability, method selection and planning was delayed. A couple of team members were eager to start on the architecture diagrams so that began later in the week.

^ Winter Break Week 2: 08-12-2022
The following week, Risk Assessment was completed and Method Selection and Planning began. Assets were considered an important factor to investigate early on so these were also experimented with. Next week a Winter Break was scheduled for the team.

^ Winter Break Week 3:15-12-2022
Asset packs and part of the method selection were completed. More time was given to finish method selection and the freed up staff were assigned to experiment with LibGDX.

Winter Break Week 4: No scheduled work done during Christmas

^ Term 2 Week 0: 05-01-2023
After a break we had a catch up meeting and noted that the documentation was completed apart from Architecture and Implementation. For architecture we needed to devote some more time to throughly update this section iteratively as each implementation sprint occurs. In addition, we gave the website a much needed make over to get it into a position where we can easily update it after each sprint.

Term 2 Week 1: No scheduled work done during exam week.

^ Term 2 Week 2: 19-01-2023
After exam week, we started work on the second sprint, meanwhile reviewing policies and laws surrounding the use of assets and libraries we have used to contribute to the documentation. Final changes to Architecure were made here before the final (3 of 3) implementation sprints.

^ Term 2 Week 3: 26-01-2023
For our last full week of project work, most of our team is now focussed on the implementation. With the reaminder of the team working on quality checking documentation which makes a large proportion of the first part of the project. Meanwhile, one final website overhall in order to ensure our context is as accessible as possible. Future plans of presentation planning have also been put in place for the following week.

Term 2 Week 4: 30-01-2023
At this stage, the submission deadline is only a couple of days away and since the most recent Gantt Chart, there is only the last changes being made to the implementation and it's respective docuementation before submission.
